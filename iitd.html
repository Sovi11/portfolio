What I studied

Courses

A CS degree at IITD covers a broad base before you specialise. The courses that actually shaped how I think:

Core CS

Algorithms & Data Structures

Where rigour meets problem-solving. The foundation for thinking clearly about complexity, correctness, and efficiency in anything computational.

Core CS

Operating Systems

Built extensions on xv6 — swapping with copy-on-write, memory management. Understanding what happens below the abstraction matters.

Systems

Computer Networks

Implemented TCP over UDP from scratch — including congestion control (AIMD), packet loss simulation, and reliability mechanisms.

Systems

Parallel & Distributed Computing

Distributed maze generation using parallel BFS/DFS with MPI. Thinking about correctness and performance in concurrent systems.

Mathematics

Linear Algebra & Probability

The mathematical backbone of everything in ML and finance. Spent serious time here — it compounds.

Intelligence

Machine Learning

Took the theory seriously — statistical learning, optimisation, generalisation bounds — before touching anything applied.

Hardware

Digital Logic & VLSI

Programmed an FPGA in VHDL. Understanding the physical layer changes how you reason about latency and performance.

Theory

Databases & Compilers

How data gets stored, queried, and how languages get parsed and executed. Surprisingly useful mental models for a lot of things.

A few stories

JEE Mains · 2021

Retook it out of boredom

Got 100 percentile in the February session. Most people stop there. Retook it in August anyway — "out of extreme boredom," as he put it to the press — and got 100 percentile again. Got AIR 3 nationally. The kind of thing that tells you something about how someone is wired.

Jane Street · 2023

Missed the final interview. Got the offer anyway.

A scheduling conflict meant missing the final interview round. Rather than accepting the outcome, convinced Jane Street to conduct it online instead. Got the offer. Perseverance through process friction is a skill — and it showed up early.
