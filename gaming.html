The game

Why Clash Royale specifically

Real-time strategy with perfect information asymmetry — you don't know your opponent's deck until they play cards. Deck building, counter-meta reads, and split-second execution all matter. The ladder is a real competitive ecosystem.

The meta

12,000+ trophies

Reaching this trophy count puts you in the top percentile of global players. The ladder at this level is solved-ish — the variance is low, and consistent wins require genuine understanding of the meta, matchups, and tempo.

The thread

Gaming, trading, thinking

Every game worth playing has a meta. Understanding the meta — the optimal strategy given the current environment — and then either playing it or exploiting the people playing it, is a universally transferable skill. It shows up in markets, it shows up in poker, it shows up in how I approach new problems.

Skill

Reading systems

Every game has rules, incentives, and emergent dynamics. Finding out how those interact — faster than other players — is the actual game.

Skill

Performing under pressure

Late in a tournament, with something at stake, every decision gets heavier. Getting comfortable in that feeling is something you only learn by being in it.
