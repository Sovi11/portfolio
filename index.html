Who I am

About

I'm a quantitative trader on the Index Options team at Optiver's Mumbai office — building and running strategies where math, markets, and machines converge every day.

I studied Computer Science at IIT Delhi (class of 2025). Along the way I interned at Jane Street in Hong Kong and Optiver in Sydney — two experiences that made it clear where I wanted to be. Each pushed me further into the intersection I find most interesting: the point where financial theory gets hard, computation gets real, and abstraction meets consequence.

Outside of work, I'm thinking about AI — not just as a tool, but as a field with deep theoretical roots worth understanding from the ground up. I like building things, reading, and occasionally writing down thoughts that feel worth sharing.
